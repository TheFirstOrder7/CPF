Preamble

We, the members of the [THE BOYS (WhatsApp group created on 23/04/2022, hereinafter “group”)], driven by the noble spirit of collective progress and mutual succour, do hereby establish this Constitution to govern the functioning of the group and its dedicated Fund, aptly named the "[Consolidated Fund]."

Membership

(a): (a)¹ Any person of sound mind and character, willing to abide by this Constitution and contribute Fifty Rupees (₹50) per month to the Fund, may be admitted as a Member. (Subs. by Art. 15 18/05/2024)

(d):Anything in this shall not bar the right of the member to continue with the existing group that was created on 23/04/2022.

Legislative Council

It Comprises all members of the group, whether involved in the fund or not.

Fund Contributions and Penalties

(a): Each Member shall faithfully discharge the obligation of monthly contribution, payable by the [7th] of each month. There shall be special provision for Members making voluntary contributions other than mandatory provision.

(b):Delinquency Charges: A late payment fee of [25 percent] shall be levied on contributions received after the stipulated deadline. Persistent delinquency, defined as [3] consecutive missed payments, will result in: (i) Temporary Suspension: Loss of voting rights (only in matters related to the governance of this fund) and eligibility for loans and benefits until all outstanding contributions and fees are settled. (ii) Termination: Involuntary Membership termination after [6] months of suspension, with subsequent ineligibility for re-enrollment until all liabilities are cleared.

5A: Pursuant to the stipulations set forth in Article 5(a), voluntary contributions are encouraged through the imposition of an additional annualised remuneration commitment of 2.5 percent, in addition to the obligatory contribution already undertaken at a rate of 5 percent per annum. This results in a cumulative interest rate of 7.5 percent on said voluntary contributions.

Withdrawals and Early Termination

(a): Members may withdraw with a 1-month notice.

(b): Early withdrawal before one year incurs a 75% fee of the accumulated fund.

(c): Alternative options for financial hardship include temporary reductions or non-financial contributions.

Loan Provision

(c): Loans are based on merit, available funds, and the member’s contribution history.

(d): Prioritizes loan repayments to maintain fund sustainability.

Charitable Contributions

(b): Charitable proposals are voted on by members, with funds disbursed directly to selected causes.

Recreational Activities

(a): Social or recreational activities may be organized at the group’s discretion.

(b): Proposals for activities are decided by majority vote.

(c): Participation is voluntary and may involve additional costs.

Ratification and Effective Date

Ratified upon legislative approval and effective from 01/01/2024.

Amendments

First Constitutional Amendment, 2024:

Increases the monthly contribution to ₹100 to meet fund demands. Adjusts related rules and obligations.

Second Constitutional Amendment, 2024:

Broadens the definition of "Members" for inclusivity in lending operations.

Third Constitutional Amendment, 2024:

Repeals Articles 3A, 3B, and 3C.

Fourth Constitutional Amendment, 2024:

State and Fundamental Laws: Defines "the State" and ensures laws inconsistent with fundamental laws are void.

Amendment Procedure: Empowers the President to introduce and ratify amendments.

Presidential Election Process: Outlines the presidential election process and powers.

Supreme Court Provisions: Introduces Ad hoc Chief Justice appointments, pre-litigation mediation, and expanded Supreme Court powers.
