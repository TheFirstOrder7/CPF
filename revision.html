Executive Order: Loan Revisioning and Restructuring Scheme

Date: 5th May, 2024

Subject: Establishment of a Framework for Loan Restructuring

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Article 21 and Article 23 of the First Order Constitution, I, [President’s Name], in my capacity as the President, hereby promulgate this Executive Order to establish a comprehensive framework for the restructuring of impending loans within our jurisdiction.

Key Provisions:

  1. Loan Restructuring Proposals: Individuals facing imminent loan maturities may submit a restructuring proposal no later than five days before the scheduled maturity. The acceptance of such proposals is at the sole discretion of the President, based on prevailing economic conditions and the financial soundness of the First Order.
  2. Supplementary Development Cess: A development cess equivalent to ten percent of the accrued interest shall be imposed on all transactions related to loan restructuring under this scheme.
  3. Interest Accrual: During the extended period, interest and/or service charges, as applicable, will continue to accrue according to the original terms of the loan agreement.

This Executive Order shall take immediate effect upon promulgation and will be enforceable starting from the 5th day of May, 2024.

Signed,

[President’s Name]
President, First Order
