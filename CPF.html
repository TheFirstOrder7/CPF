Preamble

Establishes the Constitution for the group "The Boys," now called the "Consolidated Fund," focusing on collective progress and mutual support.

Membership

(a): Admission criteria include sound mind, character, and a ₹50 monthly contribution.

(d): Existing group members retain their status regardless of other articles.

Legislative Council

Comprises all members of the group, whether involved in the fund or not.

Fund Contributions and Penalties

(a): Contributions due by the 7th of each month, with provisions for voluntary contributions.

(b): Delinquency charges include a 25% late fee. Persistent non-payment results in suspension or termination.

5A: Voluntary contributions earn an additional 2.5% annualized remuneration, making the total interest 7.5% per annum.

Withdrawals and Early Termination

(a): Members may withdraw with a 1-month notice.

(b): Early withdrawal before one year incurs a 75% fee of the accumulated fund.

(c): Alternative options for financial hardship include temporary reductions or non-financial contributions.

Loan Provision

(c): Loans are based on merit, available funds, and the member’s contribution history.

(d): Prioritizes loan repayments to maintain fund sustainability.

Charitable Contributions

(b): Charitable proposals are voted on by members, with funds disbursed directly to selected causes.

Recreational Activities

(a): Social or recreational activities may be organized at the group’s discretion.

(b): Proposals for activities are decided by majority vote.

(c): Participation is voluntary and may involve additional costs.

Ratification and Effective Date

Ratified upon legislative approval and effective from 01/01/2024.

Amendments

First Constitutional Amendment, 2024:

Increases the monthly contribution to ₹100 to meet fund demands. Adjusts related rules and obligations.

Second Constitutional Amendment, 2024:

Broadens the definition of "Members" for inclusivity in lending operations.

Third Constitutional Amendment, 2024:

Repeals Articles 3A, 3B, and 3C.

Fourth Constitutional Amendment, 2024:

State and Fundamental Laws: Defines "the State" and ensures laws inconsistent with fundamental laws are void.

Amendment Procedure: Empowers the President to introduce and ratify amendments.

Presidential Election Process: Outlines the presidential election process and powers.

Supreme Court Provisions: Introduces Ad hoc Chief Justice appointments, pre-litigation mediation, and expanded Supreme Court powers.
