Collective Prosperity Fund

The Collective Prosperity Fund (CPF) is a financial initiative aimed at providing loans to individuals and entities who are typically excluded from traditional banking systems. By offering accessible credit and interest rate subvention, including up to 100% of accrued interest through schemes like President RAHA, CPF helps bridge the gap for underserved borrowers, fostering economic empowerment and financial inclusion.
